Delhi

Delhi is the capital city of India. It is situated in the northern part of the country. It is a city of historical importance. There are many historical monuments in Delhi. The Red Fort, Qutub Minar, India Gate, and Lotus Temple are some of the famous monuments in Delhi. Delhi is also famous for its street food. The city has a rich culture and heritage. The people of Delhi are known for their hospitality. The city is well connected by road, rail, and air. The Indira Gandhi International Airport is the main airport in Delhi. The city has a good network of public transport. The Delhi Metro is one of the best metro systems in the world. The city has a good education system with many reputed schools and colleges. Delhi is a vibrant city with a mix of modernity and tradition.
